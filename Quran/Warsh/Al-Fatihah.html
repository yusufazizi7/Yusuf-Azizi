Surah Al-Fatihah

← Previous Surah Next Surah →

﷽

ٱلۡحَمۡدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلۡعَٰلَمِينَ ١

[All] praise is [due] to Allah, Lord of the worlds –

ٱلرَّحۡمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ ٢

The Entirely Merciful, the Especially Merciful,

مَلِكِ يَوۡمِ ٱلدِّينِ ٣

King of the Day of Recompense.

إِيَّاكَ نَعۡبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسۡتَعِينُ ٤

It is You we worship and You we ask for help.

ٱهۡدِنَا ٱلصِّرَٰطَ ٱلۡمُسۡتَقِيمَ ٥

Guide us to the straight path –

صِرَٰطَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَنۡعَمۡتَ عَلَيۡهِمۡ ٦

The path of those upon whom You have bestowed favor

غَيۡرِ ٱلۡمَغۡضُوبِ عَلَيۡهِمۡ وَلَا ٱلضَّآلِّينَ ٧

Not of those who have earned Your anger or of those who are astray.
